## ASSUMPTION OF RISK AND LIABILITY RELEASE

I, the undersigned participant (or parent/guardian of a minor participant), acknowledge and agree to the following:

### Nature of Activities

I understand that La Jolla Freedive Club ("LJFC") activities — including but not limited to group ocean sessions, guided freediving, breath-hold training, stretching and breathing exercises, open water swimming, and related ocean-based activities — involve inherent risks that cannot be eliminated regardless of the care taken to avoid them.

### Inherent Risks

I understand that these risks include, but are not limited to:

- Drowning or near-drowning
- Shallow water blackout or loss of motor control during or after a breath hold
- Barotrauma to ears, sinuses, or lungs
- Injuries from marine life (stings, bites, abrasions)
- Hypothermia or cold water shock
- Physical injury from surf, currents, rocks, or underwater hazards
- Equipment failure or misuse
- Exhaustion, dehydration, or overexertion
- Other participants' actions or negligence
- Injury during travel to or from dive sites

### Voluntary Participation

I voluntarily choose to participate in LJFC activities with full knowledge of the inherent risks. I accept and assume all risks of injury, illness, disability, or death that may arise from my participation.

### Release of Liability

I hereby release, discharge, and hold harmless La Jolla Freedive Club, Joshua Beneventi, and any of their respective employees, agents, contractors, volunteers, or assigns (hereinafter referred to as the "Released Parties") from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, or causes of action arising out of or related to any loss, damage, or injury — including death — that may be sustained by me or my property as a result of participation in LJFC activities, whether caused by the negligence of the Released Parties or otherwise.

### Fitness to Participate

I declare that:

- I am in good physical and mental health and capable of participating in the described activities
- I am not under the influence of alcohol or any substance that would impair my ability to safely participate
- I have disclosed any medical condition that may affect my safety or the safety of others during these activities
- I am a competent swimmer capable of swimming at least 200 meters non-stop without assistance

### Medical Authorization

In the event of an emergency, I authorize LJFC staff to arrange for emergency medical treatment on my behalf. I understand that any medical costs incurred are my responsibility.

### Media Release

I grant LJFC permission to use photographs or video taken during activities for promotional purposes, unless I notify LJFC in writing prior to the activity that I do not consent.
